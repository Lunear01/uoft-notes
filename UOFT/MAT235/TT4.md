## Parametric Functions
- The idea of having a single input `t` often referred to as time, and gives multiple outputs in terms of t, thus having something like 
	- $x = x(t)$
	-  $y = y(t)$
	-  $z = z(t)$
- Note that it starts at the origin and it has a direction
